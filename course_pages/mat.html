The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

For MAT133Y1: tutorials will begin during the fourth week of classes. The list of room assignments will be posted on the course website.

For MAT135H1: tutorials will begin during the fourth week of classes. The list of room assignments will be posted on the course website and on the MAT135H1 notice board on the ground floor of Sidney Smith Hall on the Thursday of the third week of classes.

For MAT137Y1: after September 26 tutorial enrolment and changes cannot be done on the SWS; contact the department starting September 27 for tutorial enrolment or changes. Tutorials will begin during the third week of classes. The list of room assignments will be posted on the course website and on the MAT137Y1 notice board outside SS1071 on the Friday of the first week of classes.

For MAT157Y1, 240H1, 247H1, 257Y1, 267H1 and 347Y1: Tutorials are built into the course schedule as listed in the timetable.

For MAT223H1/224H1: tutorials will begin during the third week of classes. The list of room assignments will be posted on the MAT223H1/224H1 notice board outside SS1071 and on the course website, at the end of the second week of classes.

For MAT329Y1, E indicator: students should obtain an application from BA6290 during the period of April 4 to May 10.

For all other E indicator courses: contact Donna Birch/reception at Bahen Building, Rm 6290.

Department website: www.math.utoronto.ca
