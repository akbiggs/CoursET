For RLG210Y1/SOC250Y1: Tutorials will be held Wednesday 5:00 p.m. Details will be announced in class. There will be no tutorial the first week of class.

For E indicator courses: please see the undergraduate section of the Religion website at www.religion.utoronto.ca .
