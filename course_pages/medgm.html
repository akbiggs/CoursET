Molecular Genetics and Microbiology [MGY courses - includes MIJ485H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

For AE indicator courses: students who miss the July 20, 2011 registration deadline should contact the instructor directly.

For PE indicator courses: students should contact the instructor directly starting the open round of enrolment.

For MGY376H1: this course lasts 24 weeks, the 1-hour class (M11) is held in both terms. If enrolment is high, P0201 T9-12 (Fall), T2-5 (Winter), will be added. In the Fall session, all students are in the same lab meeting section (T9-12), in the Winter session students are in one of two lab meeting sections (T9-12, T2-5), this division is necessary because of availability of specialized equipment.

For MGY480Y1: students must contact the Course Coordinator, Prof. Andrew Wilde, andrew.wilde@utoronto.ca, before September 16.
