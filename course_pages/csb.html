Cell and Systems Biology [CSB courses - includes BIO130H1, 230H1, 255H1, 260H1, 270H1 and 271H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

For BIO130H1: Alternating practical start dates have not been determined. Check the web page for more information, www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/courses/100. Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday, January 1. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO130H1S/230H1S Office (ES 3053) starting the week of January 2.
An alternating pattern to permit practicals on the same day but alternating weeks with CHM138H1S/CHM139H1S or PHY152H1S cannot be provided at this time. Please check the Office of the Faculty Registrars timetable website, www.artsci.utoronto.ca/current/undergraduate for selecting lab sections in these courses before course enrolment. Updated information will be provided before course enrolment starts.

For BIO230H1: Practicals (laboratories) meet in alternate weeks (A):PRACTICAL meeting section numbers that end with 1 start the week of September 19; PRACTICAL meeting section numbers that end with 2 start the week of September 26. Check portal for more information. Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Monday, September 5. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO130H1/230H1/BIO255H1 Office (ES 3053) starting September 6 until September 15.
For your BIO230H1 laboratory classes, if you plan to take any other course laboratory on the same day in alternating weeks, check the start dates.

For BIO255H1: Practicals (laboratories) meet every week and start the week of September 12. Check portal for more information. Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Monday, September 5. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO130H1/230H1/BIO255H1 Office (ES 3053) starting September 6 until September 15.

For BIO260H1: Tutorial enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday, January 8. Late tutorial enrolment or tutorial changes must be done in the BIO260H1 Office (RW 424E) after this date.

For BIO270H1: Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday, September 11. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO 270H1/271H1 Office (RW 424E) after this date.

For BIO271H1: Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday, January 1. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO 270H1/271H1 Office (RW 424E) after this date.

For CSB325H1: Students must register for one of the tutorial sessions which meet every third week.

For CSB349H1: enrolment in a tutorial section is done on the SWS until Monday, September 5. Late tutorial enrolment or changes must be done in the CSB349H1 Office (ES 3053).

For CSB352H1: The lecture for all students is on Thursdays at 4:00 and will be approximately 30 minutes in length. The lectures will be recorded so that students in the Friday lab who may not be able to attend the Thursday lecture, can watch the lecture before their lab on Blackboard. All students will be doing the same labs for the same length of time on Thursdays and Fridays in spite of the discrepancy in the number of hours listed.

For CSB445H1, 474H1, 483H1 and 484H1: students must submit an application form to the CSB Undergraduate Office (RW 424) by Friday, June 24.

For CSB497H1 F, 498Y1 and 499Y1: students must submit a completed CSB 497H1/498Y1/499Y1 contract to the Undergraduate Office (RW 424) by Wednesday, September 21, before being registered.

For CSB497H1 S: students must submit a completed CSB497H1 contract to the Undergraduate Office (RW 424) by Wednesday, January 18, before being registered.

For PE indicator courses: after the priority period has ended, obtain approval in the Cell and Systems Biology Undergraduate Office (RW 424). Forms for all courses are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms.

Departmental Hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 1 p.m. and 2:00 to 5:00 p.m.
