Diaspora and Transnational Studies [DTS courses]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

For program inquiries and student counselling, please contact Antonela Arhin, Executive Officer, via email, www.utoronto.ca/cdts/undergraduate.htmlalong with further instructions. The deadline for submitting the form is September 23, 2011 for F and Y courses, and January 20, 2012 for S courses.

For PE indicator courses: During the priority period, July 5 to August 3, students who are in the DTS grogram will be given priority in terms of enrolling in one of the DTS400-level courses. During open enrolment, August 7 to September 25, PE course enrolment is administered by the Centres Executive Officer, Antonela Arhin. Students who need to take one of the DTS 400-level courses to graduate in 2011-2012 are urged to enrol early. Kindly send an email to Anonela Arhin at cdts@utoronto.ca providing the following information: whether you are in the DTS major or minor program, a list of courses taken thus far, the course you want to enrol in and your student number. The deadline for submitting this information is September 23, 2011 for F courses and January 20, 2012 for S courses.
