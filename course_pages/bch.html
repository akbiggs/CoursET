Biochemistry [BCH courses - includes BCB]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

Every course in the Department of Biochemistry has either prerequisites or exclusions. Please do not enrol in any Biochemistry courses until you have consulted the course description in the Arts and Science Calendar. Students will be removed from the course if they do not have the proper prerequisites or if they have completed an exclusion course. For students who are not in one of the listed indicator programs (consult the Arts & Science Registration Handbook & Timetable) and wish to take a Biochemistry course, please contact Jennifer Haughton; email jennifer.haughton@utoronto.ca.
