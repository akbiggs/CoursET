Victoria College Courses [VIC courses - includes JSV262H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

For JSV262H1 'E' indicator course: students must follow procedures as listed in the Calendar. Application material must be submitted to P. Chandrasegaram, VC103 by the April 29 deadline for Fall enrolment.

For Vic Pathways courses, 101H1, 102Y1, 103Y1, 104H1, 106H1, 107H1, 108H1, 109H1, 112Y1, 113H1, 115H1, 118H1, 119H1, 135H1, 141Y1, 'RP' indicator courses: restricted to 1st Year Arts & Science Students (Victoria College). On August 5 priority exists for 1st Year Arts & Science Students (St. George Campus). These courses are for first year students only.

For VIC One courses, 150Y1, 151Y1,162H1, 163H1, 164H1, 165H1, 170Y1, 171Y1, 181H1, 183H1, 184H1 and 185H1, E indicator courses: students must register with the Registrars Office at Victoria College, NF106, Monday to Friday, 9:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.

For VIC220Y1, 320Y1 and 323Y1, 'R' indicator courses: students must be registered in the Semiotics program (Minor, Major, Specialist).

For VIC223Y1: this course is an introduction to Semiotics and is designed for students with less than 8 credits.

For VIC260H1 and 261H1, 'E' indicator: students must be registered in the Minor in Education and Society Program.

For all other 'E' indicator courses: students must follow procedures as listed in the Calendar. Application material must be submitted to L. Welsh, VC105 by the April 29 deadline for Fall enrolment.
