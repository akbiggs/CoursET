Laboratory Medicine and Pathobiology [LMP courses]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

For LMP365H1, LMP404H1, LMP406H1 and LMP415H1, PE indicator courses: Non-Pathobiology Specialists may be considered for enrolment. If space allows, students with similar backgrounds may be given special permission to enrol. For further information and/or instructions, contact the LMP Undergraduate Office, Medical Sciences Bldg (MSB), room 6209. (Earliest consideration for enrolment starts in June until August).

For LMP405Y1 (Project in Laboratory Medicine & Pathobiology): contact the LMP Undergraduate Office, Medical Science Bldg, room 6209 or visit the departmental website http://www.lmp.facmed.utoronto.ca/undergrad/artsc/lmp405y.htm .

For PE indicator courses: contact the LMP Undergraduate Office prior to August 7, Medical Science Bldg (MSB), room 6209.

For E indicator courses: contact the LMP Undergraduate Office prior to the end of August, Medical Science Bldg (MSB), room 6209.
