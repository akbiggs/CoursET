Psychology [PSY courses]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

The Department of Psychology website ( www.psych.utoronto.ca ) contains course details and updates, as well as a listing of academic advising office hours.

There is no prerequisite for PSY100H1, however, all PSY courses beyond PSY100H1 require prerequisites. Please consult the Arts and Science Calendar or the Department of Psychology website for details. Transfer, Non-degree, and Visiting Students must bring a photocopy of their transcripts to the Undergraduate Advisor during academic advising hours before enrolling in any PSY course at the 200-level and above if they have completed the prerequisites at another institution. This is to ensure that the Department knows you have the appropriate equivalent prerequisites for the course(s) you wish to enrol in. Failing to do so may result in your removal from the course(s). Students who have taken the prerequisites at the University of Toronto, including the UTSC and UTM campuses, need not provide such proof as this information will be available to the Department via ROSI.

For PSY400Y1 and 409H1: All 4th year Research Specialists will be added to these courses by default. No request to enrol is needed.

For PSY405H1 and 406H1: Project proposals must be submitted by the last week in August for F and Y section code courses, and by the first week in January for S section code courses.

For PE indicator courses: Enrol via the SWS during the St. George Campus round of enrolment. Starting August 10, PE course enrolment and cancellation is processed by the Undergraduate Advisor in SS4014 during regular academic advising office hours.

Departmental Hours (September 6 to 9): Tuesday to Friday, 9:30 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. and 2:00 to 4:30 p.m.
