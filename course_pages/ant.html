Anthropology [ANT courses - includes ARH, JAL355H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

For ANT100Y1: please enrol in a mandatory tutorial section through Blackboard.

Most 200 level ANT courses and some 300 level courses have tutorials/practicums. Please register through SWS for the tutorials/practicums, as applicable. There will be NO tutorials during the first week of classes.

For most 400 level courses, Priority (P), will be given to students registered in Anthropology Specialist Programs during the priority period, July 5 to August 3.

For E indicator courses: see the Undergraduate Coordinator, Anthropology Building, 19 Russell Street, Room 258, Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. and 2:00 to 5:00 p.m.
