Centre for Jewish Studies [CJS courses]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

All majors, minors and specialists in Jewish Studies are required to take CJS200H1 or CJS201H1.

The Centre for Jewish Studies has agreements with many departments about priority enrolments. Contact our undergraduate co-ordinator, cjs.undergraduate@utoronto.ca for details.

For E indicator courses: contact the Centre for Jewish Studies, Sidney Smith Hall, room 5016.
