The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

For PHY131H1 F: Students may add a practical (P) section using the SWS until September 11. Starting September 12, adding or changing Practical sections must be chosen in person in room MP 129 of the Physics Department. Practicals meet every week. Students can not alternate their practical, on the same day, with any 100-level BIO or CHM course lab. All term tests will be held on a Tuesday from 6:00 to 8:00 p.m. The times will be confirmed on the portal course sites in early September 2011. Students should not schedule tutorials/practicals in other courses in that time slot. If a conflict with another lecture is unavoidable, students should be available Tuesdays 4:00 to 6:00 p.m. to write at the alternate sitting.
