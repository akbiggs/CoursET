Rotman Commerce [RSM courses - includes MGT201H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

RSM (Rotman School of Management) courses above the 100-level are restricted to students in Rotman Commerce. Consult the Rotman Commerce website at www.utoronto.ca/rotmancommerce/timetables_enrolment.html for details. The only exception is MGT201H1 (Introduction to Financial Accounting). Enrolment in this course is restricted to Actuarial Science, Employment Relations, and Woodsworth HRM certificate students.

St. George Campus Rotman Commerce students are given priority in enrolling in RSM courses according to the Faculty of Arts & Science enrolment dates for degree students. St. George Campus Arts and Science students in an approved subject post other than Rotman Commerce (such as Woodsworth Certificate and Employment Relations students) will be eligible to enrol in 200, 300 & 400 level RSM courses (pending class space) starting on Friday, July 22, 2011 at 4:00 p.m. For all RSM 200, 300 and 400 level courses, please refer to the Rotman Commerce website, at www.utoronto.ca/rotmancommerce/timetables_enrolment.html for timetable information.

Students are responsible for fulfilling prerequisites and co-requisites; students enrolled in courses for which they do not have the published prerequisites will have their registration in those courses cancelled at any time without warning. Students must also observe exclusions. Failure to meet these requirements may result in academic difficulties. If students withdraw from a course they must also withdraw from any course for which it is a co-requisite. Students who complete a prerequisite at another institution MUST submit a transcript directly to Rotman Commerce, 125 St. George Street, prior to enrolling in RSM courses. Failure to do so will result in removal from the courses. Transcripts can be faxed directly to 416-978-5844.

Please note our new address: 125 St. George Street, Toronto, Ontario, M5S 2E8, Canada
