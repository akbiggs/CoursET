English [ENG courses - includes JEI206H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

Students will be removed from courses if they fail to note the prerequisites and exclusions that apply. 400-level courses: During the priority period, July 5 to August 3, fourth-year Specialists are allowed to enrol in only 1.0 400-series ENG FCE and fourth-year Majors who require a 400-level ENG course to complete their 2010 or later program are allowed to register in only 0.5 400-level ENG FCE. Students in other programs or years of study may register in 400-level ENG courses after the priority period ends. Exceeding these limits will result in removal from the extra courses.

For ENG201Y1: students enrolled in L0201 ONLY are to enrol in a tutorial section, T0101 F9 or T0201 F10.

For ENG202Y1: students enrolled in L0101 ONLY are to enrol in a tutorial section, T0101 to T0208.

For ENG434H1: a $100 ancillary fee will apply.

For E indicator courses: students must visit www.english.utoronto.ca for information, and then submit an application or proposal form by May 13 to V. Holmes, Department of English, 10:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. and 2:00 to 4:00 p.m.

PLEASE NOTE THAT OUR COURSES REACH CAPACITY QUICKLY.
