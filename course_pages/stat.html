The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

Tutorials begin the second week of classes. Locations will be posted on Blackboard by the instructor or TA. If the students need to change a tutorial, please use theSWSthrough September.

For AE indicator courses: students should enroll directly into the course and contact the instructor to receive approval, Department of Statistics 100 St. George St, Room 6018.

For E indicator courses: students should obtain application forms from the Department of Statistics, Sidney Smith Hall, 100 St. George Street, Room 6022.
