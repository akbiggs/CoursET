Woodsworth College Courses [WDW courses - includes Academic Bridging Program (ENG185Y1, JWH100Y1 and JWU100Y1)]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

For ENG185Y1, JWH100Y1, JWU100Y1, 'E' indicator courses: These courses are available only to students in the Academic Bridging Program. Students must enrol in these courses in person at Woodsworth College, 119 St. George Street.

'S' section code courses run from January 9 to June 8, 2012.

Departmental Hours (September to June): Monday, Wednesday and Thursday, 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.; Tuesday, 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.; and Friday, 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

WDW Courses with R, E indicators are open only to program students. It is not possible to accommodate requests from non-program students. Students must meet course prerequisites as listed in the Faculty of Arts and Science Calendar.

For WDW E indicator courses: obtain an enrolment form from the Program Office website www.wdw.utoronto.ca .

For program information or any other inquiries, visit the Woodsworth College website www.wdw.utoronto.ca .

Departmental Hours (July & August): Monday to Thursday 10:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Friday by appointment only; Program Office, Room 236, Woodsworth College. Telephone Hours: Monday to Wednesday 9:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. 416-978-5783.
