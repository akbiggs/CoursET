The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2011-2012 Registration Handbook.

For DRM200Y1: admission is based on an audition and an interview. Auditions are held in mid-April. For those students who cannot attend April auditions another set of auditions is held in August. Applicants must submit a DRM200Y application form by March 1st.

For DRM228H1: admission is based on a portfolio submission after the completion of DRM100Y1. The deadline for submission is May.

For DRM254Y1: admission is based on an interview in April after the completion of DRM100Y1. Applicants must submit a ballot form by March 1st.

For DRM354Y1: admission is based on an interview in April. Applicants must submit a ballot form by March 1st.

Please note: Performance classes, DRM200Y1, 300Y1 and 400Y1 require a Voice co-requisite (DRM201H1, 301H1, 401H1) and a Movement co-requisite (DRM211H1, 311H1, 401H1). The section a student is placed in for a Performance class must be the same section for their Voice and Movement co-requisites Both Voice and Movement classes include mandatory tutorials which will be held up to 3 times each term per student.

For DRM 'E' indicator courses: complete a DRM200Y1 application form or a ballot form to schedule an audition and/or interview and see the Program Assistant, UC Drama Program, 79 St. George Street, 416-978-8099. Applications and ballot forms are due March 1st and can be obtained from the departmental office or from the website: www.uc.utoronto.ca/drama.

For the latest updates and additional information on the Program, consult our web page at www.uc.utoronto.ca/drama .

For PCS260Y1, 360Y1, 460H1 and 461H1, 'PE' indicator courses: as of August 9, Departmental approval is required. Please contact Megan Ball, Program Administrator, pcs.programme@utoronto.ca, 416-946-8917.

For further information, contact the Trudeau Centre for Peace and Conflict Studies Program Office, pcs.programme@utoronto.ca , 416-978-2485 (t), 416-946-8915 (f).

For UNI 'E' and 'PE' indicator courses: College approval is required. Please contact Khamla Sengthavy in UC 173, Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. for further information.
